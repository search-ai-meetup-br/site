# Search & AI Meetup BR

**Search & AI Meetup BR** is a Brazilian community created to promote knowledge sharing, learning, and collaboration around **Information Retrieval, Search Engineering, and Applied Artificial Intelligence**.

This repository contains the source code for the official landing page of the community.

## About the Community

Search & AI Meetup BR was founded to connect people who work, study, or are interested in search technologies and applied AI.

Our goal is to build a strong, open, and active Brazilian community where ideas, experiences, and knowledge can be shared in a respectful and collaborative environment — both online and in-person.

## What We Explore

As a community, we focus on topics such as:

- Search systems and ranking
- Information Retrieval
- Embeddings, RAG, and LLM-based applications
- Applied Artificial Intelligence
- Research papers, tools, and real-world case studies

## What We Do

Search & AI Meetup BR exists to:

- Share knowledge, papers, tools, and experiences  
- Encourage discussion and exchange between industry and academia  
- Connect people through networking and collaboration  
- Organize online and in-person meetups across Brazil  

This community only makes sense with active participation. Everyone is welcome to ask questions, share ideas, suggest topics, present projects, or simply follow the discussions.

## Events & Call for Papers

We regularly organize meetups focused on Search and Applied AI.

Community members are encouraged to participate by:
- Attending events
- Submitting talks and proposals through our **Call for Papers**
- Suggesting venues and partners for future meetups

All event-related links and forms are shared through the official landing page and community channels.

## Code of Conduct

Search & AI Meetup BR is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone.

All participants, speakers, organizers, and partners are expected to follow our Code of Conduct.

Read the full Code of Conduct in [`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
